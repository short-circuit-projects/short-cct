The Brief

Innovate. Build. Document.

Level up your baseline. Your mission is to iterate on a standard Short Circuit project to solve a complex engineering problem, optimize its performance, or engineer a completely original extension.


Examples of Acceptable Modifications:

Current Version Design Challenge Modification
Basic local smartwatch with limited networking Cloud connected smartwatch with live notifications
Basic ball and beam with PID controller and EMA filtering Ball and Beam with Kalman filtering and advanced controllers
Basic lane-tracking autonomous car Car reactive to stop signs on the race track
Basic pick and place robotic arm under uncertain conditions Robotic arm able to pick and place objects of various colors and sizes

Judging Criteria

Problem Framing (20%)

Does the project show a clear problem definition, explicit constraints, and measurable success criteria?

Technical Execution (30%)

Does the project include a clean structured implementation and clear technical depth?

Creativity (30%)

Does the project try an experimental or novel method to change or improve their system?

Documentation (20%)

Does the project include documentation outlining the competencies from the rest of the rubric?
