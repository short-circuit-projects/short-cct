Controller Implementation Demo

Record and submit a video demonstrating your completed Ball and Beam controller. This submission shows that you have successfully integrated sensing, filtering, PID control, and actuation into a working closed-loop system.

Video Requirements

  • System Overview - Brief view of your hardware setup showing the beam, ball, sensor, and motor
  • Ball Stabilization - Demonstrate the ball being stabilized at the center position
  • Disturbance Recovery - Push the ball and show it returning to center automatically
  • Setpoint Changes (Optional) - If implemented, show the ball tracking different positions
  • Video Duration - 1 to 3 minutes in length with brief verbal or text explanation
